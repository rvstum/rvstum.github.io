# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** February 6, 2025

## Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how KDAssist uses cookies and similar tracking technologies.

## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They enable websites to remember user preferences and track site usage.

## Types of Cookies Used

### Essential Cookies

Required for basic site functionality:

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| session_id | Maintains authenticated sessions | Session-based |
| auth_token | Validates user authentication | 30 days |
| user_preferences | Stores site preferences | 1 year |

These cookies cannot be disabled without preventing core functionality.

### Analytics Cookies

Used for site performance analysis via Google Analytics:

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| _ga | Distinguishes users | 2 years |
| _gid | Distinguishes users | 24 hours |
| _gat | Throttles request rate | 1 minute |

Analytics cookies collect anonymous data including:

- Page views and navigation patterns
- Session duration
- Approximate geographic location
- Browser and device information

Personal information (name, email, account data) is not collected by analytics cookies.

## Managing Cookies

### Disabling Analytics Cookies

**Browser Extension:**  
Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on:  
https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

**Browser Settings:**  
Configure cookie preferences in your browser:

- Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and site data
- Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
- Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Cookies

### Disabling All Cookies

Disabling all cookies will prevent:

- User authentication
- Preference storage
- Normal site operation

Essential cookies are required for KDAssist to function.

## Third-Party Cookies

KDAssist only uses third-party cookies from Google Analytics. We do not use:

- Advertising networks
- Social media trackers
- Marketing platforms
- Any other third-party tracking services
